You're Note Alone

Applying for SSDI or SSI for the first time can be confusing, but you’re not alone. You may qualify if a medical condition keeps you from working. The process involves forms, medical records, and deadlines. London Disability guides you through each step of the application process to help make it easier.

Understand Your Eligibility

Your Medical Condition

Avoid These Common Mistakes on Your First Application

Mistakes to Avoid

  • ▪ Incomplete or Inconsistent Information

    Missing or conflicting details in your application can cause automatic denials.

  • ▪ Missing Deadlines for Information Requests

    The SSA has strict deadlines and missing them can lead to a denial.

  • ▪ Underestimating Key Medical Evidence

    Weak or limited medical evidence is one of the most common reasons for denial.
